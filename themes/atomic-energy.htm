Atomic energy


Mechanics working on the reactor at Dounreay Atomic Power Station in November 1959.
©TopFoto

During World War Two, Britain cooperated with the US on the Manhattan Project and produced the first atomic bombs. The British government expected collaboration on atomic energy to continue but, in 1946, the American McMahon Act forbade the sharing of atomic information.
