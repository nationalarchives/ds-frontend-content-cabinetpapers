The Attlee years


A snowbound bus near Oldham in February 1947 - the extreme weather conditions reflect post-war austerity.
©TopFoto

Following the Labour election victory in 1945, the trade unions collaborated with the Government in economic and social policy until 1951. This moved Britain in the direction of corporatism.
