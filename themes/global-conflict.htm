A global conflict


The Camel Corps, deployed on guerilla operations in Egypt and Palestine in 1918, was made up of diverse commonwealth troops.
©Imperial War Museum Q105525

The First World War was a global conflict. By the time the War Cabinet came into being, the war was not going well for the British and colonial troops in Egypt, Palestine or Mesopotamia.
