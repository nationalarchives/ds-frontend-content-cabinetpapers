Government and industry in the 1970s


James Callaghan, former Labour Party Leader and Prime Minister addresses party members.
©TopFoto

During the 1970s, government policy for industry was aimed at increasing efficiency, innovation and the formation of larger business units. Economic developments in the mid-1970s, however, imposed limits on policy.
