China until 1950

In 1931 General Yorita watches the entry of Japanese assault troops into Manchuria.
©TopFoto

During the first half of the 20th century the empire in China exercised economic power without formal political control. The major exception was Hong Kong, which had been a British Crown Colony since 1842.
