Freedom of Information Act

The Freedom of Information Act (FOI) was passed in November 2000 and came fully into effect from 1 January 2005. FOI has changed how records are made available to you, and how we respond to enquiries about the archives.

Visit The National Archives' pages on the Freedom of Information Act for more information.
