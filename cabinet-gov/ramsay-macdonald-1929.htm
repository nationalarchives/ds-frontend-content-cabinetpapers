Ramsay MacDonald 1929

Labour Government
5 June 29 - 24 August 31

Although the 1929 election did not produce an outright majority for any party, the Labour Party won under Ramsay MacDonald for the second time. With 288 Labour MPs to the Conservative Party's 260 and the Liberal's 59, the second Labour government was again forced to rely on opposition parties. The government was elected just months before the Wall Street Crash and was therefore forced to cope with global depression. The pressure of keeping to the economic orthodoxy required by international financiers and other governments became too much, and the government disintegrated under the strain.
