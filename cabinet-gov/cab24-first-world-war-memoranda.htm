CAB 24 First World War memoranda

Between 1916 and 1919 the War Cabinet memoranda were referenced using an internal reference and consecutively numbered papers; G 220 referred to the 220th paper put before the War Cabinet in series G, while GT 1 referred to the first paper of series GT put before the War Cabinet. The internal reference for this period is G (1-266) for the period 1916 to 1919, and GT (1-8412) for the period 1917 to 1919. Part of this series also features a sub-series of Reports that are listed separately below.
