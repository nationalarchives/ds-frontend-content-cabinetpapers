CAB 65 Second World War conclusions

From 1939 to 1945 the internal reference for Cabinet conclusions changed to WM. Despite the introduction of a smaller War Cabinet, the notation used to identify the meetings and conclusions remained the same. WM 10 (43)1 refers to the 10th meeting in 1943 and the first agenda item or conclusion.
