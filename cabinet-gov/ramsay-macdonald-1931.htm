Ramsay MacDonald 1931

Labour Government
24 August 1931 - 7  June 1935

After the collapse of the Labour government, King George V asked Ramsay MacDonald to form a new National Government. Following an election held in October 1931 to provide the new government with a mandate, the vote returned 554 MPs under this coalition, drawn from the Conservatives, Labour and various Liberal factions. MacDonald's Cabinet was made up of a large number of Conservative MPs and a smaller number of Labour and Liberal MPS. It responded to the desperate financial situation by making massive cuts in government expenditure.
