Harold Wilson 1974

Labour Government
4 March 1974 - 4 May 1979

The first of two elections in 1974 failed to secure a majority for any one party but Labour took power under Harold Wilson. A further election was called in October of that year when Labour achieved a majority of three seats. Wilson's government faced international recession. A referendum in 1975 about Britain remaining in the Eorpean Economic Community (EEC) prevented an internal split within the Labour party.
